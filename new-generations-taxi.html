New generation's taxi

We shook Serbia out of «polako» by speaking to the generation tired of old-school taxis. With Miloš as our nightlife hero, we turned real frustrations into OLVs, OOH and TikTok hits — boosting awareness, users, and finally giving young Serbs a way to move at their own pace.

Client: Yandex Go. Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Anya Kanikovskaya, Vasiliy Podtynnikov, Sasha Klimovskiy. Role: Art-director. Year: 2025.
