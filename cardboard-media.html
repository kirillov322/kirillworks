Cardboard media

Covid-19 pandemic was the worst time for homeless people. We have turned their signs from cardboards to boxes with orders from Yandex Market. After your order is arrived you can send things back to charity for free.

Client: Yandex Market. Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Olga Veretinskaya, Tatiana Kolotilova, Daria Kosacheva. Role: Art-director. Year: 2021. Recognitions: Eurobest Bronze.
