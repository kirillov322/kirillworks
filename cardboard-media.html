Cardboard media

COVID-19 has been a very difficult time for the homeless. There were fewer people on the streets and no one noticed their calls for help. So we decided to use the surfaces of Yandex Market boxes to deliver messages from the cardboard signes of homeless. We also added a feature, so people can reuse this boxes to send clothes and other basics to the homeless for free.

Client: Yandex Market. Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Olga Veretinskaia, Tatiana Kolotilova, Daria Kosacheva. Role: Art-director. Year: 2021. Recognitions: Eurobest Bronze, ADC Awards 2 Gold cubes, The One Show 3 merits.
