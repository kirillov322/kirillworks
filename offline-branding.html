Offline branding

Yandex is № 1 IT company in Russia runnig more than 7 different offline services. The brand become ultra visible with over 1 million cars, couriers and dark stores across the country. Our challenge was to develop a flexible design system which helps main brand be recognizable and sub-brands to display their identity.

Client: Yandex. Team: Vasiliy Podtynnikov, Sergey Puchkin, Vanya Kazukov, Oksana Ivanova. Role: Lead Designer. Year: 2021.
