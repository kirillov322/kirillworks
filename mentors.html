Driver's mentors

Mentors are the top rated taxi drivers, helping beginners kickstart their work in the Yandex Taxi. They are very proud of their status, so we’ve decided to enhance this feeling and created a simple logo and uniform to present mentors themselfs as the main identity element.

Client: Yandex Pro (App for taxi drivers). Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Vladimir Kulikov, Sasha Klimovskiy, Liza Dovzhik. Role: Designer. Year: 2020.

In Russian Mentor called Наставник (Nastavnik). Logo based on the first letter H.
