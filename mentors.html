Driver's mentors

Mentors are the top-rated taxi drivers, helping beginners kickstart their work in Yandex Taxi. They are very proud of their status, so we have decided to enhance this feeling and create a simple logo and uniform to present mentors as the main identity element.

Client: Yandex Pro (App for taxi drivers). Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Vladimir Kulikov, Sasha Klimovskiy, Liza Dovzhik. Role: Designer. Year: 2020.

In Russian Mentor called Наставник (Nastavnik). Logo based on the first letter H.
