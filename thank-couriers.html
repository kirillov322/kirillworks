Thank couriers

While everyone stayed home during COVID-19, Yandex Eats couriers kept the city running. Our response? Showing courier names after each delivery and creating a video about city life continuing at home. Thousands shared their gratitude to these everyday heroes on social media.

Client: Yandex. Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Lilia Popovskaya, Tatiana Kolotilova, Olga Veretinskaya, Liza Dovzhik. Role: Designer. Year: 2020. Recognitions: Big Picture Festival Bronze.
