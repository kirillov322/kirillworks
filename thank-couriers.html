Thank couriers

A large campaign of thankfullness to the heroes of COVID-19 — Yandex Eats couriers included the «Life in the city continues at home» video and a simple symbol, with which thousands of people shared their thanks on social networks.

Client: Yandex. Agency: Magic Camp (in‑house). Team: Lilia Popovskaya, Tatiana Kolotilova, Olga Veretinskaya, Liza Dovzhik. Role: Designer. Year: 2020. Recognitions: Big Picture Festival Bronze.
